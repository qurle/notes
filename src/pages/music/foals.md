# Foals

![Foals live 2022](https://www.cleveland.com/resizer/v2/WNA3EXA4EREFVKWJUIOVXSB7FE.jpg?auth=d16606b1ec391272d3f42ad5f1a3b9d2b97a31b6bfacb2997b4a2c89a702a1c3&width=1280&smart=true&quality=90)

Foals are a British rock band formed in Oxford in 2005. Greek-born vocalist Yannis Philippakis fronts a lineup that made its name on polyrhythmic post-rock before expanding into arena-filling art rock.

## Sound

Their debut *Antidotes* arrived steeped in math rock — Yannis' guitar locked against Jack Bevan's stuttering drums in a style influenced by Don Caballero and Battles. With each record they shed complexity for weight: *Holy Fire* introduced arena dynamics, *What Went Down* opened with one of the most brutal riffs of the 2010s, and the *Everything Not Saved* diptych earned them their first UK #1.

> "We want to be bigger than Radiohead, bigger than Oasis. I'm not ashamed to say that."
> — Yannis Philippakis
