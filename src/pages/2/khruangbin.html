Khruangbin

Khruangbin (Thai: เครื่องบิน, meaning "airplane") are an American musical trio from Houston, Texas, formed by bassist Laura Lee, guitarist Mark Speer and drummer Donald "DJ" Johnson. Their largely instrumental sound draws on Thai funk, Iranian pop, dub and soul.

The band has released four studio albums: The Universe Smiles Upon You (2015), Con Todo El Mundo (2018), Mordechai (2020) and A La Sala (2024), alongside collaborative EPs with soul singer Leon Bridges.
