# **Foals**

![Foals 2022](https://www.cleveland.com/resizer/v2/WNA3EXA4EREFVKWJUIOVXSB7FE.jpg?auth=d16606b1ec391272d3f42ad5f1a3b9d2b97a31b6bfacb2997b4a2c89a702a1c3\&width=1280\&smart=true\&quality=90 "Foals 2022")

Foals (stylised in [all caps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/All_caps "All caps")) are a British [rock](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rock_music "Rock music") band formed in [Oxford](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oxford "Oxford") in 2005. The band's current line-up consists of Greek-born lead vocalist and guitarist [Yannis Philippakis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yannis_Philippakis "Yannis Philippakis"), drummer and percussionist Jack Bevan, guitarist and keyboardist Jimmy Smith, and bassist Walter Gervers.

***

Foals are currently signed to [Warner Records](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Warner_Records "Warner Records"),[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foals_\(band\)#cite_note-1) and have released seven studio albums to date: *[Antidotes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antidotes_\(album\) "Antidotes (album)")* (2008), *[Total Life Forever](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Total_Life_Forever "Total Life Forever")* (2010), *[Holy Fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Fire_\(album\) "Holy Fire (album)")* (2013), *[What Went Down](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/What_Went_Down "What Went Down")* (2015), *[Everything Not Saved Will Be Lost – Part 1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everything_Not_Saved_Will_Be_Lost_%E2%80%93_Part_1 "Everything Not Saved Will Be Lost – Part 1")* and *[Part 2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everything_Not_Saved_Will_Be_Lost_%E2%80%93_Part_2 "Everything Not Saved Will Be Lost – Part 2")* (2019), with the latter becoming the group's first album to top the [UK Albums Chart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UK_Albums_Chart "UK Albums Chart"), and their most recent, *[Life Is Yours](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Life_Is_Yours "Life Is Yours")* (2022). They have also released one [video album](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_album "Video album"), six [extended plays](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extended_play "Extended play") and 35 [singles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Single_\(music\) "Single (music)").
