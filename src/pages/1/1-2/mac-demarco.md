# **Mac DeMarco**

![Mac DeMarco](https://picsum.photos/seed/mac-demarco/1280/720 "Mac DeMarco")

Mac DeMarco is a Canadian singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist known for his lo-fi, "jangly" guitar pop and a self-deprecating stage persona he has called "jizz jazz". Originally from [Duncan, British Columbia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duncan,_British_Columbia "Duncan, British Columbia"), he records most of his music alone at home.

***

His catalogue includes *[2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2_(Mac_DeMarco_album) "2")* (2012), *[Salad Days](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salad_Days_(album) "Salad Days")* (2014), *[This Old Dog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/This_Old_Dog "This Old Dog")* (2017) and *[Here Comes the Cowboy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Here_Comes_the_Cowboy "Here Comes the Cowboy")* (2019). In 2023 he surprise-released *One Wayne G*, a five-and-a-half-hour collection of unreleased recordings, for free.
