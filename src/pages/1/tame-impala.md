# **Tame Impala**

![Tame Impala](https://picsum.photos/seed/tame-impala/1280/720 "Tame Impala")

Tame Impala is the psychedelic music project of Australian multi-instrumentalist [Kevin Parker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kevin_Parker "Kevin Parker"), who writes, performs, records and produces every album alone in his home studio near [Fremantle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fremantle "Fremantle"), Western Australia. Live performances feature a full backing band.

***

The project's blend of psychedelic rock, synth-pop and dream pop spans four studio albums: *[Innerspeaker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Innerspeaker "Innerspeaker")* (2010), *[Lonerism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lonerism "Lonerism")* (2012), *[Currents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Currents_(Tame_Impala_album) "Currents")* (2015) and *[The Slow Rush](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Slow_Rush "The Slow Rush")* (2020). Parker has also written and produced for artists including Lady Gaga, Kanye West and Travis Scott.
