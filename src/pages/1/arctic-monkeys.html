Arctic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002, consisting of Alex Turner (lead vocals, guitar), Matt Helders (drums, backing vocals), Jamie Cook (guitar) and Nick O'Malley (bass guitar).

Their debut album, Whatever People Say I Am, That's What I'm Not (2006), became the fastest-selling debut album in UK chart history at the time. Subsequent releases Favourite Worst Nightmare (2007), Humbug (2009), Suck It and See (2011), AM (2013), Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino (2018) and The Car (2022) have each topped the UK Albums Chart.
